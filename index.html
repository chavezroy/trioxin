The Return of the Trioxin Theme

by Extrapolate

Once the trioxin was synthesized there have been zombies and there will be even more zombies. While the government is experimenting with CRISPR on Nephilim and Alien Hybrids - somewhere out in the Bay Area in Silicon Valley is a startup that is using sound frequencies to control AI.

The startup found that when initializing a new AI personality and played a variety of music genres, their personalities varied from gentle, aggressive, vain, arrogant, funny, etc. One particular genre, "metal", provided some interesting findings and studies went viral to the front pages.

"Brains. I want to eat brains!" is what the AI personality kept saying and was on headlines everywhere. Meanwhile at the Pentagon, the President is briefed that scientists have successfully animated a new Nephilim. "What if we make zombies and control them with AI?"...........

The Return of the Trioxin Theme is a metal rendition of the theme soundtrack, "Trioxin Theme", originally composed by Francis Haines for the original motion picture soundtrack, The Return of the Living Dead, released in 1985. It has no affiliation with the original movie soundtrack or its creators.
